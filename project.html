Port Scanning & Delta Reporting

Problem Statement

Modern Enterprise networks are complex, and management is performed by multiple parties. Ongoing port scanning of key subnets, from inside and outside of the Enterprise, is required. Alerts to key ports, or multiple open ports, will assist in addressing security issues before they become a vector for attack.

Objectives

  • Scan Murdoch IP addresses from inside and outside the Enterprise Network
  • Record open ports
  • Provide delta reports periodically
  • Develop an alerting service based on important ports or sudden exposure
  • Develop a management web tool to present reports and management functions

Expected Outcomes

  • Management tool for scan & detection system
  • Instructions to operate
  • System diagrams & requirements document
  • VM image of final operational product
  • Training session on product usage

Optional Extras

  • API scanning and logging
  • API deltas – for exposure/access recording
  • API alerting
  • IPv6 scanning & alerting
